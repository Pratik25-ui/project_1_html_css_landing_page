About

Misophonia India is an initiative dedicated to spreading awareness about misophonia, both within India and across the world. This page was created to provide reliable information, share experiences, and build a supportive community for individuals who live with sound sensitivity. By starting conversations and promoting understanding, we hope to reduce stigma and help people realize they are not alone. Our vision is to create a space where awareness leads to acceptance, empathy, and meaningful change.
